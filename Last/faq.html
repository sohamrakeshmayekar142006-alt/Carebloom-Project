Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about donating and using CareBloom.

How do I donate items?

To donate, simply sign up as a donor, click "List an Item," and fill out the details about what you'd like to give away. NGOs can then see your listing and request it.

What kind of items can I donate?

You can donate gently used items like clothes, books, toys, stationery, and small household goods. Please ensure all items are clean and in good condition.

Is there a cost to use CareBloom?

No, CareBloom is completely free for both donors and NGOs. Our mission is to make giving as easy and accessible as possible.

How do I choose which NGO receives my donation?

When an NGO requests your item, you will receive a notification. You can view the NGO's profile and mission before deciding to accept their request.
